About Urban Gardening

By giving Filipinos access to fresh, nutritious food, it addresses the problem of food insecurity that exists in urban areas. As individuals work to promote sustainable agriculture in their communities, urban food production—especially when done by a group—can also aid in reducing the negative consequences of climate change.
